About Us
Our Story

Born From a Suitcase Crisis.

Rentacation started the way most great ideas do — from a real problem. Our founder spent three hours the night before a dream vacation staring at a closet, overwhelmed, and still ended up overpacking every wrong thing.

The lightbulb moment: travelers aren't bad at fashion. They just lack a guide. No one was combining local knowledge, climate research, and real styling expertise to help people get dressed for vacation.

We launched in 2022 with a simple premise — fill out a form, get three perfect outfits. The waitlist filled within days. Within six months, we had styled over 300 trips across 30+ destinations. Travelers stopped dreading packing and started loving it.

Today we're a team of passionate stylists, avid travelers, and sustainability advocates who believe your wardrobe shouldn't add to your pre-vacation stress — it should be the fun part.
